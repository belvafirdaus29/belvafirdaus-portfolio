Project 01 / Golden Project

Merchandise visuals for official apparel, department jackets, and student campaigns.

Project Overview

Golden Project is a student-led merchandise initiative within the department focused on producing and promoting official apparel and event-related products. The project included selling Telkom University uniforms and department jackets.

My Role

Contributed to visual design for promotional and merchandise materials, designed digital assets for ordering and campaign purposes, and supported social media content creation.

Design Process

The work centered on turning merchandise information into clear campaign visuals: product emphasis, pre-order messaging, social feed consistency, and supporting assets for ordering flows.

Tools Used

Adobe Photoshop, Canva

Final Result

A coordinated set of merchandise posters, Instagram feed/story content, Google Form design support, and campaign visuals for student fundraising.
